Why the Brave browser?

Simply put, Brave is a browser for the people. It blocks malicious ad trackers and pop-ups out-of-the-box and has it's own crypto token baked in called Basic Attention Token (BAT). Using BAT within Brave, you can actually get paid for watching ads in BAT and support your favorite websites by donating BAT to them. For more information about BAT, Coinbase will pay to learn about it.
